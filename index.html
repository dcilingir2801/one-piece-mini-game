Story

Ace has been captured by the marines. The world government sees him as a threat as he is the only son of Gol D. Roger, the late King of the Pirates, and wants to execute him publicly at Marineford. Help Luffy to save his sworn brother!

Instructions

Move up and down to navigate Luffy through Marineford
Fight the Marines trying to stop him but make sure not to injure any allies
Each fight drains Luffy's energy, so make sure to pick up any meat on the way
