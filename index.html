Story

Ace has been captured by the marines. The world government sees him as a threat as he is the only son of Gol D. Roger, the late King of the Pirates, and wants to execute him publicly at Marineford. Help Luffy to save his sworn brother!

Instructions

Use your keyboard to navigate Luffy through Marineford
Avoid the Marines: each collision will make you lose one of your three lives
Collect Allies: whenever you spot an ally, go and collect them - you win if you collect ten in total


Change the course of history - save Ace!
